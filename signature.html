About Us

A trusted engineering solutions provider

Energy King Engineering specializes in Vertical Transportation Systems, Smart Building Technologies and Industrial Engineering Solutions. Established in 2016, we deliver reliable, safe and energy-efficient solutions through collaboration with internationally recognized manufacturers and technology partners.

Our expertise combines quality engineering, innovative technology and professional project management — serving residential, commercial, industrial, healthcare, hospitality and government sectors.

We believe an elevator is more than transportation. It is part of modern living — daily convenience, accessibility, passenger safety and long-term value. We provide complete project support from consultation and planning to installation, commissioning and after-sales service.

Safety & Technology

Engineered for safety and comfort

Every system is built around passenger safety, smooth operation and energy efficiency.

Automatic Rescue Device (ARD)

Safety during unexpected power failure:

  • Backup battery activates automatically
  • Elevator moves to the nearest floor
  • Doors open automatically
  • Passengers can exit safely

VVVF Drive Technology

Comfort in everyday life:

  • Smooth acceleration & stopping
  • Reduced vibration, low noise
  • Accurate floor leveling
  • Enhanced passenger comfort
